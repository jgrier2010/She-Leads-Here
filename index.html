Questions

The honest answers.

What if I can't make the Thursday Conversations live?
Every Conversation is recorded and added to the library. You can watch on your time, in your timezone — and our community is built across four continents, so async is the norm, not the exception.
How is this different from Chief, Hampton, or Ellevate?
Chief is gender-only and white-default. Hampton requires you to be a founder. Ellevate is broad-corporate. She Leads Here is the only premium leadership community built specifically by and for Black women — at every stage, in every industry, anywhere in the world.
What happens to my founding rate if I cancel and rejoin?
Honestly: you'd lose it. Founding rates are locked for life only as long as you remain a member. That's the deal — we keep our coaches paid because our founding members stay.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly members can cancel any time. Annual members get a 7-day refund window, no questions asked. After that, your annual term runs to its end date.
Do I have to be a senior executive to join?
No. We have founders, VPs, mid-career professionals, and women navigating major transitions. The common thread is ambition, ownership, and willingness to show up for other women in the room.
How do I book a session with a coach?
Truthbearer members get 4 banked 15-min sessions per year, redeemable with any coach in the directory. Wayfinder members can book sessions directly with coaches at their published rates. The full coach directory is searchable by specialty, location, and language.
